K Game Development Club

Purpose

A club for game developers from any grade or skill-level. Work together with other members to develop or plan out games, whether that game is the club's or your own personal project. We hope to help develop your skills in whatever field you want to be in (programming, art, 3D modeling, game design, etc.).

We will mainly work with Godot and Roblox Studio* to create video games, big and small, along with modular systems to use as building blocks for future games. In terms of programming/scripting, we will mainly work using Luau and GDScript. However, we are also open to other programming languages and game engines.

*Roblox as a platform has many issues with moderation and child safety. This club uses Roblox Studio as an educational resource to learn to create a game and does not condone any actions of the company itself. It is important to be aware of the ethical and moral issues surrounding both Roblox and the game industry as a whole. For those who aren't comfortable with using Roblox-related programs, I recommend using/learning Godot.

Resources

Whether you're just starting out or have been in the field for a bit now, we are actively providing resources to game developers to help with developing their games. Ranging from progroamming help to launching a game, we want to support projects that every one of our members want to make.
